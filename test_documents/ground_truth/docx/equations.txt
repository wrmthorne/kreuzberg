The angle is $\theta = \sin^{- 1}\left( 2.2 \times 10^{- 4} \right) = 2.2 \times 10^{- 4}rad$.

$$\frac{a}{\lambda} = \frac{m}{sin\theta} = \frac{1}{sin{45.0}^{\circ}} = 1.41.$$
